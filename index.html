About Me

1. Custom Fiberglass Paddleboard

Overview: Designed and built a custom fiberglass paddleboard using composite materials for lightweight, durable performance.

Challenges: Working with fiberglass to ensure a smooth finish and structural integrity.

Outcome: Successfully created a board ideal for high-performance use, learning advanced composite techniques.

2. Composite Ping Pong Paddle

Overview: Developed a high-performance ping pong paddle with composite materials and a 3D printed grip.

Challenges: Designing for grip and balance while ensuring durability.

Outcome: Produced a functional and ergonomic paddle that improves playability.

3. Automated Chicken Coop Door

Overview: Designed an Arduino-controlled automated door system for a chicken coop, opening and closing based on time.

Challenges: Ensuring reliability and smooth operation in all weather conditions.

Outcome: A fully functional automated door system, making poultry management easier.

Notes: I do not have many pictures of the system because the door is now dismantled so that the Arduino UNO can be repurposed for other projects. My family also likes to open the coop themselves. 6. Crawfish Monitoring Project

Outcome: Created a fully functional prototype that monitors key environmental factors.

Outcome: Secured winning position by delivering a high-performing robot.
